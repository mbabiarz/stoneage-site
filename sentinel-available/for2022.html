Sentinel Availability

2022 Supply Chain Update

StoneAge recently launched the AutoBox 3L with Sentinel Automation Technology in more regions globally, with the goal of helping our customers perform critical cleaning applications safer, faster, and more profitably. However, like many companies around the world, StoneAge has been impacted by recent supply chain shortages — specifically, the scarcity of certain semiconductor chips.  

Despite these challenges, our commitment remains to enable our global customers to benefit from Sentinel Automation Technology to the greatest extent possible. Accordingly, StoneAge has decided to suspend unit sales and instead expand our Sentinel rental fleets and rent-to-own options. We will consider fulfilling orders on a case-by-case basis as availability permits. AutoBox 3L owners will be able to convert their systems to operate with Sentinel technology once more chips become available in 2023.

To ensure you are both well-equipped and well-informed, we’ve compiled the FAQ below detailing system availability. If you have any additional questions or feedback, please reach out to your RSM.  

Frequently Asked Questions

How is StoneAge combatting supply chain issues?
StoneAge will not sit idle and wait for the supply chain to improve. To deliver on our promise of helping customers complete their jobs efficiently, safely, on time, and on budget, we are having daily check-ins with our global suppliers and our in-house electrical and firmware experts are working on overdrive to creatively produce more chips for Sentinel units.
When will the Sentinel-enabled AutoBox 3L be available for purchase?
We expect to receive a larger supply of semiconductor chips in the beginning of 2023. Purchase availability for the Sentinel-enabled AutoBox 3L is pending these supply chain delivery dates.
Where can I rent a Sentinel-enabled AutoBox 3L?
You can rent the Sentinel-enabled AutoBox 3L through our branch offices in Texas, Louisiana, Ohio, the UK, and the Netherlands. Please contact your RSM for more information.
Can I purchase or rent a Sentinel through a dealer?
Dealers will be equipped to sell, rent, and support Sentinel beginning in 2023. This allows time to build their understanding of the Sentinel and thoroughly train staff on how to successfully support the system.
How do I qualify to Rent-To-Own (RTO) the Sentinel-enabled AutoBox 3L?
Anyone in designated regions can be eligible to rent-to-own the Sentinel. Please contact your RSM for more information about this option.
If I am already renting an AutoBox 3L with Sentinel on an RTO contract, can I apply my spending to date on the purchase of new system once they become available?
Current spending may only be applied to the purchase of the system on the given contract. However, your Sentinel-enabled system will continue to receive firmware updates as they are released. This will keep your contracted system up to date with the latest features.
Can I submit a P.O. to secure my place in line to purchase a new system once available?
Yes, purchase orders will be prioritized per the order of which they are received.
Will I be billed on my preorder at the time of shipping or upon receipt of the P.O.?
Sentinel preorders will be billed at the time of shipping.
Will StoneAge increase pricing for the Sentinel in 2023? 
No, StoneAge commits to holding the price of the Sentinel system at its current MSRP through 2023.
If I wait until 2023 to purchase a Sentinel, will there be advancements in Sentinel technology, or will it be the same?
All customers will continue to have access to firmware updates as they become available, regardless of when you purchase a Sentinel system. With the Sentinel, you can always count on having the latest technology features.
Given the shortage, will StoneAge be able to support existing Sentinel units in the field?
Yes, StoneAge is taking measures to ensure we support our valued partners who own or rent a Sentinel unit. This includes setting aside numerous systems specifically to support units in the field as needed.
The Sentinel-enabled AutoBox 3L has been delayed a few times. Is there anything functionally wrong with the system that is impacting its availability?
Absolutely not. Global semiconductor chip shortages are impacting every company that uses electronics, and StoneAge is not exempt from this ongoing issue. The Sentinel is a high-quality, high-performing system that’s backed by the StoneAge Assurance Promise and trusted by numerous early-adopters worldwide.
