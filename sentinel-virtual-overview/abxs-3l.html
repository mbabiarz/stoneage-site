The Sentinel-enabled AUTOBOX 3L is a CE-certified system consisting of a modern controller, sensors, and a power hub that integrates with StoneAge’s AutoBox 3L tractor system to enable automated exchanger tube cleaning.

Sentinel's advanced productivity features drastically speed up the cleaning process to deliver an unmatched operational experience and stellar cleaning results.

Power Hub



The Power Hub seamlessly converts electronic signals from the controller to drive the AutoBox 3L Sentinel system throughout operation.​ Designed with maximum durability for harsh waterblasting conditions, the PowerHub is water resistant and has a sealed design separating the electronic and pneumatic elements.  

  • Features an integrated FRL (filter, regulator, and lubricator) to aid in valve pack and air motor life​
  • Eliminates caps and plugs​ with a door that covers fittings and connectors
  • Provides designated compartment to securely store controller when not in use

The Power Hub is equipped with Short Circuit Protection to automatically reset​ all supply side connections, as well as a kill switch to safely and instantly cut the power supply.

Positioner



The Lightweight Positioner with Sentinel Automation Technology (LWPS) is an indexing system engineered to safety and efficiently index across heat exchanger tubes. The positioner's sensors track the location and advancement of the tractor in order to consistently locate the next set of tubes across the heat exchanger face.

  • Lightweight and versatile design
  • Assembly​ requires no specialized tools
  • Metric hardware​ available

The LWPS features push-connect air supply​ fittings and Sentinel sensors on each drive carriage. The positioner drive carriages have been designed with tighter tolerances in order to produce improved accuracy.​

Tractor

The AutoBox 3L tractor with Sentinel Automation Technology (ABXS-3L)is an automated system for hands-free heat exchanger tube cleaning applications. The Sentinel-enabled tractor consists of sensors that are added to the front and back of the machine to detect location, feed rate speeds, feed sequence, and limit the ability to index the tractor while hoses are in the exchanger. The tractor sensors track whether lances are fully inserted or retracted.  

  • Performs triple, dual, or single lance operations
  • Feeds and retracts lances with the click of a button
  • Handles hose sizes from 3/2 to 8/4

With proven reliability in the most destructive environments, the portable, modular design of the Sentinel-enabled AutoBox 3L ensures effortless transport and setup between jobs.
