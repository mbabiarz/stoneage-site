SL Swivel

Share
  • E-mail link

The SL Swivel was designed to be a versatile, compact union for use in conveying high pressure fluid from a stationary line to a rotating or twisting assembly.

Due to the unique seal design, the torque necessary to rotate the swivel is relatively small, even at high pressures. At 10,000 psi only 5 in-lb is required for rotation.

Features

  • Heavy-duty stainless steel construction
  • Unique, leak-free seal design requires very little torque for rotation
  • Multiple inlet options available mean the tool can be used at different pressures
  • External grease fittings for easy maintenance
  • 0° or 90° inlet connection simplifies hose routing

Applications

  • Preventing hose kink
  • Conveying high pressure water from a stationary line to a powered rotating or twisting assembly
  • Standard equipment on Rigid Lance Machines
