BPT-100 Banshee ProTex

Share
  • E-mail link

The BPT-100 Banshee ProTex Safety Stop is a lightweight, durable splashguard built to keep operators safe from the potential hazards of manual flex-lancing. The ProTex is designed specifically for use with Banshee tube cleaning tools and the anti-withdrawal collet easily adjusts to fit many hose sizes. A removable insert easily centers smaller tools and we offer an optional three foot long tube for use with a stinger.

Features

  • Protects the hose sheath as well as the operator during tube cleaning operations
  • Ideal for vertical exchanger applications
  • Accomodates a range of hose sizes
  • Small tool centering device for optimal cleaning
