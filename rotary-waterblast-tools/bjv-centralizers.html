BJV Centralizers

A centralizer balances jet standoff distance for more consistent cleaning and helps to protect the tool as it passes through the pipe.

In cases where pipe size is more than 1.5 times the diameter of the tool, a centralizer is an important safety device, preventing the tool from turning around and thrusting backwards out of the pipe.

  • Optimize jet cleaning for small to very large pipes
