Descaling Heads

Share
  • E-mail link

StoneAge offers optional heads that allow you to change our standard nozzle configuation. Your existing Wartjog jetting tool can easily be converted into a powerful descaling tool by simply changing the head.

The descaling pattern features two additional jets at 105° combined with a plugged front port to ensure maximum power. The result is enhanced removal of scale, tuberculation, corrosion products, soap and minerals.

TECH TIP: We recommend plugging the front jets to help maximize descaling and pulling power when using this option.

Features

  • Optimized jet pattern for polishing and descaling
  • Available for multiple inlets
