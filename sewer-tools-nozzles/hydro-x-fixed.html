Non-Rotary Hydro-X

Share
  • E-mail link

Hydro Excavation is a safe alternative for providing access to buried utilities by potholing or trenching with high pressure water.

StoneAge Non-Rotary Hydro-X nozzles utilize replaceable 1/8” NPT AP2 Attack Tip nozzles (HX-102/103 models) or a single OCIH 1/4” NPT carbide nozzle (HX-101). Using a single nozzle channels all of your pump's power in one direction for the most digging power, whereas the inline 2 and 3 jet patterns produce a wider spray to trench material faster.

Features

  • Single, double or triple jets — for precise and accurate soil removal
  • 1/2” NPT inlet connection to attach to the operating wand
  • High-visibility urethane guard
