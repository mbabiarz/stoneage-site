Hydro Excavation is a safe alternative for providing access to buried utilities by potholing or trenching with high pressure water. StoneAge now has four options available for different applications.

Features

  • Angled rotating jet pattern on rotary model removes more material and safely cleans around utilities
  • Save labor hours and expensive heavy equipment costs
  • Avoid risks of costly and dangerous damage to existing utilities
  • Replaceable shroud made from UHMW
  • Rebuild kits available
