WHR Switcher

Share
  • E-mail link

The WHR Switcher is two tools in one. Switch between pulling or cleaning without removing the nozzle from the pipe. When the pump is idled down and brought back to pressure, the tool will switch the water flow between two different sets of jets.

The WHR Switcher features the same technology as the WGR Switcher for 3/4" inlets.

Features

  • Eliminates extra runs needed to swap nozzles, saving water and time
  • Excellent for longer runs, climbing hills, going against the flow and flushing large amounts of material without affecting residential infrastructure
  • Run more jobs with one tank of water
  • Switch to pulling jets for maximum thrust
  • Switch to cleaning jets maximize power for cutting roots annd clearing debris
