Industrial Theory Podcast

Listen to Episode 24 - What to Expect at IFAT 2022 with the Exhibition Director, Philipp Eisenmann

Guest:
Philipp Eisenmann is the Exhibition Director of IFAT Munich, the world’s leading tradeshow for water, sewage, waste, and raw materials management.

Episode in a Tweet: After a long pandemic-caused hiatus, IFAT is back in action! Hear from Exhibition Director, Philipp Eisenmann on how to take advantage of everything being offered at the upcoming show in Munich, Germany.

Summary: In this episode of Industrial Theory, host Kerry Siggins is joined by Philipp Eisenmann, the Exhibition Director of IFAT. Held in Munich from May 30th – June 3rd, IFAT is the world’s largest tradeshow for wastewater, environmental, and industrial technologies. Kerry and Philipp discuss the organization’s 50-year history, from its start in 1966 to becoming a globally recognized brand with major events in Shanghai, Mumbai, Johannesburg, and Istanbul (just to name a few). Philipp shares what visitors can look forward to at the upcoming show, including conference programs specific to the Future of Water, Circular Economies and Resource Efficiency, Sustainable Cities and Municipalities, as well as Intelligent Drives and Zero Emissions. Plus, Philip details a new option for visitors to have a personalized guide help find a specific solution within the nearly 3,000 exhibitors at the event.

For more information about IFAT and the upcoming event in Munich, visit https://ifat.de/en/.
