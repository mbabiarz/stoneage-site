Custom engineered waterblasting solutions for your most challenging jobs

The safety and efficiency benefits of automated waterblasting for industrial cleaning demonstrate why these methods are quickly becoming standard operation. With proper equipment and practices, tough jobs can be accomplished faster, with measurably better results and fewer safety incidents.

Bridging the gaps

We believe that the successful adoption of automated waterblasting technology requires a partnership between contractors, asset owners, and manufacturers of waterblasting equipment. StoneAge Solutions can help asset owners understand the benefits of adopting automated waterblasting solutions and assist industrial cleaning contractors improve their effectiveness by successfully engaging plants. We partner with all parties to provide job assessments, on-site plant visits, presentations, and live demonstrations of hands-free, automated equipment.

Book a fully equipped StoneAge product demonstration in the US or Europe and get access to all of StoneAge’s latest equipment and tooling.

Contractor Support

The StoneAge Solutions team offers many services to support industrial cleaning contractors, including safety, product and application training, technical support on hard-to-clean applications, and Most Effective Technology (MET) reports. MET reports provide a comprehensive review of the plant-specific waterblasting system and recommendations for safety and efficiency improvements.

Learn more about the StoneAge Training program.

Custom Engineered Solutions

The StoneAge Solutions team can design custom engineered solutions to help contractors and plants clean the most difficult applications. Our custom solutions range from simple product modifications to comprehensive turnkey packages. Our process allows for close collaboration between our engineering team, the contractor, and the plant, leveraging four decades of global engineering experience.
