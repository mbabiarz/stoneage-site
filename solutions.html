Partnerships that work: Asset Owners, Contractors, Equipment Manufacturers

The safety and efficiency benefits of automated waterblasting for industrial cleaning demonstrate why these methods are quickly becoming standard operation. We believe that the successful adoption of automated technologies requires a partnership between contractors, asset owners, and manufacturers of waterblasting equipment. StoneAge Solutions can help asset owners understand the benefits of adopting automated waterblasting solutions and assist industrial cleaning contractors improve their effectiveness by successfully engaging plants. We partner with all parties to provide job assessments, on-site plant visits, presentations, and live demonstrations of hands-free, automated equipment.

Contractor Support

The StoneAge Solutions team offers many services to support industrial cleaning contractors, including safety, product and application training, technical support on hard-to-clean applications, and Most Effective Technology (MET) reports. MET reports provide a comprehensive review of the plant-specific waterblasting system and recommendations for safety and efficiency improvements.
