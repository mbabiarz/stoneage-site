AutoBox ABX-3L

Share

The AutoBox ABX-3L is an automated system for hands-free heat exchanger tube cleaning applications. It is designed to run triple, dual, or single flex lance setups with an emphasis on portability, ease of setup, and safety.

Features

  • Triple, dual, or single lance operation
  • Handles hose sizes from 3/2 to 8/4 without component changes
  • Steel-reinforced drive belt is good for hundreds of hours of operation and easy to replace
  • Independent forward/reverse speed control and remotely adjustable hose clamp pressure allows operator to adapt to changing conditions and minimize hose wear
  • High quality construction to withstand corrosive environments
