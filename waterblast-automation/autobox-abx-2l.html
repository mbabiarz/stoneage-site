AutoBox ABX-2L

Share

The StoneAge AutoBox ABX-2L is an automated system for “hands-free” heat exchanger tube cleaning applications. It is designed with an emphasis on safety, ease of setup, portability and reliability. When paired with StoneAge Banshee rotary tube cleaning nozzles and lightweight positioner, the AutoBox ABX-2L offers safe and efficient tube cleaning in the harshest exchanger environments.

Features

  • Single or Dual lance capability
  • Lightweight, modular design for single operator use
  • Accommodates hose sizes from 3-8 mm
  • Adapts to changing conditions and minimizes hose wear
  • Withstands corrosive environments

ABX-2L Training

What items are required for a complete working configuration of the AutoBox ABX-2L?

  • ABX-2L hose tractor
  • Lance guide assembly
  • Guide tubes
  • Backout collet
  • Hose feed stop
  • Control box

You may also need the following:

  • Positioner, with appropriate length of drive rail
  • Positioner mounting clamps
  • Flex lances
  • Rotary nozzles
  • Hose feed stop
  • Y manifold

What information is needed in order to properly configure a working system?

  • Tube diameter/Size of rotary tool
  • Size of hose
  • Diameter of tube bundle ( to determine positioner window size)
  • Length of guide tubes required (Standard 12.5” and 36” lengths, custom lengths also available)
  • Preferred mounting clamp
