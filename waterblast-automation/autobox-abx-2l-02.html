AutoBox ABX-2L

The StoneAge AutoBox ABX-2L is an automated two lance system for hands-free heat exchanger tube cleaning applications. It was designed to reliably control one or two flex lances with an emphasis on portability, ease of setup, and safety.

The ABX-2L can be used with any pressure rated hose ranging in size from 3/2 to 8/4. Paired with StoneAge Banshee rotary tube cleaning nozzles and Lightweight Positioner, the ABX-2L offers safe and efficient tube cleaning in the harshest exchanger environments.

Features

  • Lightweight, modular design allows a single operator to move, set up and run
  • Quick-swap rollers can be changed without tools to accommodate different hose sizes
  • Hose clamp pressure and feed rates can be remotely adjusted on the fly for repeatable, consistent cleaning
  • Proven reliability on the toughest jobs and most corrosive environments
  • Low cost of ownership – low cost components, quick and easy maintenance
