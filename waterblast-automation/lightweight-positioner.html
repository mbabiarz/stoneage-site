Lightweight Positioner

Share
  • E-mail link

The Lightweight Positioner is used in conjunction with AutoBox automated flex lancing systems for controlled, efficient, and safe cleaning of heat exchanger tube IDs. It is specifically designed to be modular, lightweight, easy to setup, and expandable to allow for cleaning of different sized heat exchangers.

Features

  • Adaptable to different sizes of tube bundles
  • Lightweight 2.5" box rail construction for easy portability and good corrosion resistance
  • Quick and simple assembly
  • Clamp and bolt attachment options available
