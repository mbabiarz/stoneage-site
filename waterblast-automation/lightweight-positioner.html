Lightweight Positioner

Share

The Lightweight Positioner is used in conjunction with the AutoBox family of hose tractors for controlled, efficient, and safe cleaning of heat exchanger tube IDs. It is specifically designed to be modular, lightweight, easy to setup, and expandable to allow for cleaning of different sized heat exchangers.

Compatible with:

ABX-3L ABX-2L ABX-PRO

Features

  • Adaptable to different sizes of tube bundles
  • Lightweight 2.5" box rail construction for easy portability and good corrosion resistance
  • Quick and simple assembly
  • Clamp and bolt attachment options available
