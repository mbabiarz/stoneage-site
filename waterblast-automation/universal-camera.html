Universal Camera System

Share
  • E-mail link

Made to withstand the harshest of conditions, the Universal Camera System is an all-inclusive kit specifically designed for industrial cleaning with automated equipment. The Universal Camera System securely attaches to any automated equipment and displays a clear, up-close view of the working surface on a monitor at the control box. The ultra-durable camera provides better visibility of an operation to enable faster, safer and more precise completion.

The Universal Camera System is CE-certified and comes with everything needed for easy transport, setup and use. It provides real-time visibility during any automated cleaning application, including operations that require remote monitoring from outside a vessel such as confined space entry and tank cleaning applications.

Features

  • Universal mounting options allow one camera system to be used across an entire fleet of automated equipment
  • ‘Lobster Claw’ mount with two swivel joints for adjustability securely positions camera to equipment
  • Monitor with real-time display easily attaches to any control panel
  • Continuous power supply with EU and US connector options
  • Portable in a compact carrying case

A limited batch of camera systems is anticipated in the first quarter of 2021.
