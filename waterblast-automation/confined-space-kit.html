Features

  • Integrated camera system enables operators to precisely position equipment during low visibility conditions, without a line of sight, and without needing to enter confined spaces
  • Curved guide tube and "spider plate" clamp design allow for complete cleaning around the tube sheet face with fewer stops to reposition equipment
  • Quick set-up with a single attachment point, push-to-connect hoses, and quick clamps
